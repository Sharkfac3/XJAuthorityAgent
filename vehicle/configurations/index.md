# Vehicle Configurations Index

## Purpose
Route stock configuration matrices and baseline combinations.

## Scope
Use this branch when the question is about how factory combinations change the vehicle baseline.

## What belongs here
- engine and transmission combinations
- 2WD vs 4WD baseline differences
- ABS vs non-ABS baseline implications
- configuration matrices that cut across more than one stock system

## What does not belong here
- repair procedures
- diagnostics trees
- donor or purchase advice

## Child branches
- Expected child branches: `2wd-vs-4wd.md`, `engine-trans-combinations.md`, `abs-vs-non-abs.md`, and other stock matrix leaves
