# Vehicle Configurations Index

## Purpose
Route stock configuration matrices and baseline combinations.

## Scope
Use this branch when the question is about how factory combinations change the vehicle baseline.

## What belongs here
- engine and transmission combinations
- 2WD vs 4WD baseline differences
- ABS vs non-ABS baseline implications
- configuration matrices that cut across more than one stock system

## What does not belong here
- repair procedures
- diagnostics trees
- donor or purchase advice

## Current child leaves
- [`engine-trans-combinations.md`](engine-trans-combinations.md) — first-hop engine and transmission narrowing
- [`2wd-vs-4wd.md`](2wd-vs-4wd.md) — drivetrain-layout split before transfer-case, axle, or driveline assumptions
- [`abs-vs-non-abs.md`](abs-vs-non-abs.md) — brake-system and axle-side split when ABS presence changes the answer
