# Transmission: Manual

## Variants in scope
| Transmission | Years | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| BA10/5 | 1988–1989 | Peugeot-sourced, 5-speed. Known as weak — many replaced with AX15 |
| AX5 | 1988–1994 | Aisin 5-speed, small-block. Adequate for stock power levels |
| AX15 | 1989–2001 | Aisin 5-speed, heavier duty. Preferred manual in the XJ |

## ECU interaction
None. Manual transmissions have no TCM and do not tap any ECU signals. The engine ECU swap has no direct effect on any manual transmission.

## Swap notes
- No TPS tap, no lockup solenoid, no transmission-related wiring considerations
- Clutch switch (if present) may be wired to ECU for idle-up or fuel cut — verify and retain if used
- Neutral switch on some years — verify function and retain or replicate if ECU uses it
