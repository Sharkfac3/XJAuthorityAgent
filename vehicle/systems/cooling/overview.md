# Cooling Overview

## Purpose
Route stock Jeep XJ cooling-system baseline facts.

## Scope
Use this branch for factory cooling architecture, major split points, and stock component-family context that later maintenance, repair, diagnostics, and sourcing work depends on.

## What belongs here
- stock cooling-system layout
- fan-package and thermal-control baseline notes
- configuration or year splits that materially change stock parts or routing
- cross-links to maintenance, diagnostics, and sourcing branches that depend on this baseline

## What does not belong here
- flush, refresh, or replacement procedures
- symptom-first overheating diagnosis
- brand or donor recommendations as the primary topic
- modified-state cooling upgrades except for routing pointers
