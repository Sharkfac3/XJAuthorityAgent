# Vehicle Systems Index

## Purpose
Route stock Jeep XJ facts by subsystem.

## Scope
Use this branch when the main question is about a stock system that may cut across years, trims, or configurations.

## What belongs here
- subsystem overviews
- stock component-family baselines
- system-specific year changes
- stock operating architecture

## What does not belong here
- procedures and service workflows
- symptom-first diagnostics
- donor or purchasing guidance
- package-only content that belongs under `vehicle/packages/`
