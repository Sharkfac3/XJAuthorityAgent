# Suspension — Index

## Scope
Stock XJ suspension architecture, component specifications, and geometry baselines. What the XJ left the factory with and why those choices matter for modifications.

## What belongs here
- Stock suspension architecture and component families
- Factory year, era, package, or drivetrain splits
- Geometry baselines and how they change at lift
- Cross-links to axle, steering, and work branches

## What does not belong here
- Lift kit installation procedures → `work/upgrades/suspension/`
- Symptom-first troubleshooting → `diagnostics/`
- Donor parts or sourcing advice → `sourcing/`
- Steering components (separate branch) → `vehicle/systems/steering/`

## Leaves

- `overview.md` — system layout, axle matrix, stock ride height and ground clearance
- `front.md` — control arms, track bar, caster specs, ball joints, coil springs
- `rear.md` — leaf spring specs, shackles, add-a-leaf, SOA overview, rear axle identification
- `lift-geometry.md` — what changes at lift: track bar angle, caster, driveshaft angle, SYE, bump stops

- Steering geometry: `vehicle/systems/steering/`
