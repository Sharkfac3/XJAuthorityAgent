# Vehicle Interchange Baselines Index

## Purpose
Route factual stock interchange limits without turning this branch into sourcing advice.

## Scope
Use this branch for what fits or shares baseline family traits in stock XJ terms.

## What belongs here
- factual stock fitment baseline
- factory interchange families
- year-range interchange limits
- hardware-family compatibility notes without buying recommendations

## What does not belong here
- parts-quality recommendations
- junkyard sourcing strategy
- full installation procedures
- swap or upgrade planning
