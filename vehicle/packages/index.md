# Vehicle Packages Index

## Purpose
Route package-defined stock differences.

## Scope
Use this branch for factory package or trim groups that materially change hardware or baseline configuration.

## What belongs here
- factory package baselines
- package-linked hardware differences
- package-specific suspension, cooling, axle, or electrical notes
- future package folders such as `up-country/` or `towing-package/`

## What does not belong here
- generic system facts that belong under `vehicle/systems/`
- procedures and repair workflows
- aftermarket package-style modification guidance

## Child leaves
- [`trim-levels.md`](trim-levels.md) — complete trim level timeline (1984–2001) with mechanically significant differences; rear axle, wiring, and A/C implications by trim
- [`option-packages.md`](option-packages.md) — Command-Trac, Selec-Trac, Trac-Lok, Up-Country, tow package, cold weather group
