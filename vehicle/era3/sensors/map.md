# Era 3: MAP Sensor

## Specs
Identical to Era 2. See `vehicle/era2/sensors/map.md` for full resistance curve and voltage table.

## Connector
- 1996: Era 2 connector (Metri-Pack 150 3-pin) — see `vehicle/era3/1996-notes.md`
- 1997–2001: Metri-Pack 150 3-pin — same connector family as Era 2

## Swap notes
Same as Era 2. Chrysler 1-bar MAP is directly supported in rusEFI and Speeduino. No changes needed between Era 2 and Era 3 installs.
