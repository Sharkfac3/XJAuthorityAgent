# Radiator — OEM vs. Aftermarket (XJ Cherokee)

## What this covers
Radiator selection for the XJ Cherokee 4.0L. Does not cover installation procedure.

---

## OEM-style replacement
The stock XJ radiator is adequate for a stock engine in normal operating conditions. OEM-style plastic-tank aluminum-core replacements are available from most parts stores and work fine for a stock build.

---

## Aftermarket aluminum radiators

### Mishimoto MMRAD-XJ-89
- Fits: 1989–2001 XJ with 4.0L
- Aluminum two-row core with strutted tubes; improved cooling capacity over stock
- Correct inlet/outlet sizing and positions for XJ — confirmed fitment
- Lifetime warranty
- Community-verified choice on NAXJA and JeepForum

---

## Cheap eBay / no-name aluminum radiators — avoid
A well-documented community complaint: cheap aluminum radiators marketed for the XJ Cherokee frequently have **incorrect inlet and outlet sizing or placement**. This means hoses don't seal properly, or the radiator must be forced to fit. Mishimoto engineering posts and NAXJA threads have called this out explicitly. Spend the extra money on a confirmed-fit unit.

---

## When to upgrade from stock
- **Towing:** Stock radiator is marginal under sustained load in warm weather
- **Built engine:** Any increase in power output increases heat rejection demand
- **Hot climate operation:** Stock cooling is borderline on very hot days even in stock form; the 4.0L is known to run warm
