# CAD Delete Kit — XJ Cherokee Sourcing

## What this covers
CAD delete kit selection for the XJ Dana 30 front axle. Does not cover installation procedure.

---

## What the CAD system is
From the factory, XJ Cherokees with the Dana 30 front axle use a **Central Axle Disconnect (CAD)** system. The inner passenger-side front axle shaft is split into two pieces. A vacuum-actuated sleeve shifts to engage or disengage the two halves, allowing the front axle to be disconnected for 2WD operation without the driver getting out.

The CAD system is known to fail over time. The vacuum actuator, vacuum lines, and the disconnect sleeve are all wear and failure points. When the system fails it can leave the front axle stuck disconnected (no 4WD engagement) or stuck engaged (parasitic drag in 2WD).

---

## What a CAD delete does
A CAD delete kit replaces the two-piece inner passenger axle shaft with a **solid one-piece inner shaft**, eliminating the disconnect mechanism entirely. The vacuum system is abandoned in place or capped off.

**Result:** The front axle is permanently engaged mechanically. Front axle drag in 2WD increases slightly (similar to a manually-locking hub system without locking hubs), but the vacuum failure point is gone.

---

## Typical kit contents
- Solid one-piece inner passenger-side front axle shaft
- Replacement axle seal (inner seal for the passenger side — NAPA 11782 or equivalent; verify fitment)
- Some kits include an updated or locking outer hub

Rusty's Off-Road and Yukon Gear both offer complete CAD delete kits. Yukon documents the specific shaft and seal requirements clearly and is a good reference even if not purchasing from them.

---

## What it does not fix
- Worn or failed vacuum lines to the transfer case shift motor — those are a separate concern
- Outer axle shaft or birfield wear — the CAD delete only addresses the inner passenger shaft
