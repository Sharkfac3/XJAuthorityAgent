# SYE Kits — XJ Cherokee Sourcing

## What this covers
Slip Yoke Eliminator kit selection for the NP231 transfer case. Does not cover installation procedure.

---

## What an SYE does
The NP231 transfers power to the rear driveshaft via a **slip yoke** — a splined shaft that slides in and out of the transfer case output as the suspension cycles. At stock ride height this works fine, but when the rear of the vehicle is lifted the driveshaft operating angle increases. Once the angle exceeds the slip yoke's tolerance, the driveshaft vibrates, especially at highway speed and under acceleration.

An SYE replaces the slip yoke output with a **fixed output flange**, then pairs it with a new rear driveshaft (typically a double-cardan CV shaft from Tom Woods or similar) to handle the steeper angle cleanly.

---

## When an SYE is needed
- **Lifts over ~3.5"** combined with the stock slip yoke rear driveshaft — driveshaft vibration is the symptom
- Any lift where driveshaft vibration appears at highway speed that is not resolved by a t-case crossmember drop
- Any build going 6"+ — essentially mandatory
- Not required for 2" spacer lifts under normal use

A t-case crossmember drop (using a manual-trans crossmember on an auto XJ) can address mild vibration on 2"–3" lifts without an SYE. At 3.5"+ the SYE is the durable solution.

---

## What a kit includes
- Replacement tailhousing for the NP231 (contains the new fixed-flange output bearing)
- Fixed output flange (1310 or 1350 series depending on kit)
- Seals and hardware
- Most kits do **not** include the new rear driveshaft — that is a separate order (see Tom Woods below)

---

## Brands with NP231 XJ kits

| Brand | Notes |
|-------|-------|
| **Tom Woods (4xshaft.com)** | SYE kit plus custom-length double-cardan rear driveshaft ordered together; community standard; ships within one business day |
| **TeraFlex** | Produces NP231 SYE kits; widely used on XJ and TJ |
| **Advance Adapters** | Supplies NP231 SYE hardware; also known under SYE.com branding |

Ordering the Tom Woods SYE kit and driveshaft together is the most common community approach — the driveshaft is built to the exact length needed for the lift height, so measurement matters when ordering.

---

## Budget alternative: hack-n-tap SYE
A front driveshaft from an automatic XJ can be repurposed as the rear driveshaft in a budget SYE setup using a 3103-27CV yoke. This is a known community workaround documented in junkyard upgrade threads. It works but a proper SYE tailhousing is stronger and more reliable for long-term use.
