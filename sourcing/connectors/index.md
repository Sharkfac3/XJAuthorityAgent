# Connectors Index

## Purpose
Route connector, terminal, pigtail, and harness-end selection work.

## Scope
Use this branch when the main question is which connector family, terminal set, pigtail, or repair approach to source.

## What belongs here
- connector-family identification that supports a sourcing decision
- OEM pigtail vs aftermarket repair option comparisons
- terminal, seal, cavity-plug, and service-kit selection guidance
- links to narrower system-specific connector sourcing branches

## What does not belong here
- full depinning, repinning, or harness-build procedures
- symptom-first electrical diagnosis
- stock-only wiring facts with no selection decision
- repo governance or agent behavior
