# Junkyard Upgrades — Engine & Cooling

Donor-vehicle engine, cooling, and related components that interchange with the XJ Cherokee 4.0L.
Sourced from community-compiled junkyard upgrade threads (NAXJA, JeepForum, CherokeeForam, Pirate4x4).

---

## Cylinder Head & Intake

### 1992+ HO Head and Intake
- **Donor:** 1992+ Jeep XJ, ZJ, or TJ with 4.0L (HO / High Output)
- **Fits:** 1987–1990 Renix-era XJ
- **Gain:** Better-ported head and intake manifold — measurable power increase
- **Fitment notes:** Direct swap possible while retaining Renix ECU and sensors. The HO head flows better than the Renix casting. Requires the power steering bracket from the donor if using the later intake manifold.

### Later XJ/ZJ Intake Manifold
- **Donor:** 1991+ XJ or ZJ with 4.0L
- **Fits:** Earlier 4.0L XJ
- **Gain:** Improved intake flow
- **Fitment notes:** May require power steering bracket swap. Minor modification depending on year combination.

## Fuel Injectors

### Mustang 5.0 Injectors
- **Donor:** Ford Mustang 5.0L
- **Fits:** All XJ 4.0L years
- **Gain:** 4-hole spray pattern (vs. stock single-hole) — better fuel atomization, improved throttle response
- **Fitment notes:** Near bolt-in. One of the oldest and most widely recommended XJ junkyard upgrades. See also `vehicle/era*/injectors.md` for era-specific injector interchange details.

### Dodge Neon Injectors
- **Donor:** Dodge Neon
- **Fits:** All XJ 4.0L years
- **Gain:** Improved throttle response
- **Fitment notes:** Check flow rate against your era's stock injectors. See `vehicle/era*/injectors.md` for detailed Neon 703/784 interchange notes.

## Cooling — Electric Fan

### Ford Taurus / Mustang Electric Fan
- **Donor:** 1990–1995 Ford Taurus 3.8L or 1987–1993 Ford Mustang 5.0L
- **Fits:** All XJ years
- **Gain:** Replaces mechanical fan — frees up 5–10 HP, better cooling at idle and low speed. Dual-speed Taurus fan moves ~4000 CFM combined.
- **Fitment notes:** Ford part number 8C607 (Taurus). Requires wiring a relay and switch or temp sensor. One of the most popular XJ cooling upgrades. The Taurus fan is the community standard.

## Cooling — Overflow & Pressure Bottles

### Crown Victoria / Intrepid Pressure Bottle
- **Donor:** Late 1990s Ford Crown Victoria or Dodge Intrepid
- **Fits:** All XJ years
- **Gain:** Pressurized coolant expansion tank — cleaner installation than stock
- **Fitment notes:** Near-direct fit. Requires adapter hoses. Good upgrade when converting to open cooling system.

### MJ Overflow Tank
- **Donor:** Jeep Comanche MJ
- **Fits:** All XJ years
- **Gain:** Fender-mounted coolant overflow — cleaner routing than factory setup
- **Fitment notes:** Mounts on fender with ~18" hose. Simple and clean.

### Open Cooling System Conversion
- **Donor:** Dodge Durango or Dakota (4.7L radiator fill cap)
- **Fits:** All XJ years
- **Gain:** Converts closed cooling system to open — easier to bleed, easier to service
- **Fitment notes:** Cut the Durango/Dakota fill cap fitting into the XJ upper hose. Replace the stock bottle with an MJ overflow tank or any generic overflow. No radiator replacement needed.

## Fuel Cell / Tank

### ZJ or 97+ XJ Fuel Cell
- **Donor:** 1993–1998 Jeep Grand Cherokee ZJ or 1997–2001 XJ
- **Fits:** XJ (in-cab or custom mount)
- **Gain:** Plastic fuel cell with pump assembly — accepts stock XJ fuel pump
- **Fitment notes:** Used for in-cab fuel cell builds or custom tank relocations. Not a direct bolt-in as a stock tank replacement.

## Oil Breathers & Separators

### Small Block Chevy Oil Breather
- **Donor:** Any small block Chevrolet (valve cover breathers)
- **Fits:** XJ 4.0L (with PCV-sized adapter)
- **Gain:** Chrome breather caps — cosmetic upgrade and improved ventilation
- **Fitment notes:** Custom fit to PCV-sized openings on the 4.0L valve cover.

### Pontiac Quad 4 Oil Separator
- **Donor:** Mid-1990s Pontiac with Quad 4 engine
- **Fits:** XJ 4.0L
- **Gain:** Finned aluminum oil separator with return line — reduces oil vapor in intake
- **Fitment notes:** Plumb between valve cover breather and intake. Includes oil return line back to the crankcase.

## Hood Vents

### LeBaron / Laser Hood Vents
- **Donor:** Chrysler LeBaron or Plymouth Laser
- **Fits:** All XJ years
- **Gain:** Under-hood heat extraction — helps cooling at low speeds and idle
- **Fitment notes:** ~$12 each at junkyards. Cut and bond into XJ hood. Cosmetic and functional.
