# Junkyard Upgrades — Body & Interior

Donor-vehicle body and interior parts that interchange with the XJ Cherokee.
Sourced from community-compiled junkyard upgrade threads (NAXJA, JeepForum, CherokeeForam, Pirate4x4).

---

## Doors & Glass

### 1997+ XJ Doors
- **Donor:** 1997–2001 Jeep Cherokee XJ
- **Fits:** 1984–1996 XJ
- **Gain:** One-piece side glass (no vent window frame) and updated mirror mounts
- **Fitment notes:** Direct bolt-on to older XJs. Gives a cleaner look and eliminates the vent-window leak point.

## Mirrors

### 1997+ XJ Side Mirrors
- **Donor:** 1997–2001 Jeep Cherokee XJ
- **Fits:** 1984–1996 XJ
- **Gain:** Larger mirror surface and fold-flat capability for tight trails
- **Fitment notes:** Direct bolt-in swap to older XJ doors.

## Seats

### MJ / 2-Door XJ Bucket Seats
- **Donor:** Jeep Comanche MJ or 2-door XJ Cherokee
- **Fits:** 4-door XJ (at least older models) and MJ
- **Gain:** Tilt-forward seat backs — easier rear access
- **Fitment notes:** Direct bolt-in. The 2-door/MJ buckets have a tilt mechanism that the 4-door seats lack.

### ZJ / WJ Seats
- **Donor:** 1993–2004 Jeep Grand Cherokee ZJ or WJ
- **Fits:** XJ (with bracket work)
- **Gain:** Leather seats, more bolstering, upgraded comfort
- **Fitment notes:** Get the mounting brackets with the seats. May require some drilling for alignment. The ZJ seats are closest in width.

## Console

### 1997+ XJ Center Console
- **Donor:** 1997–2001 Jeep Cherokee XJ
- **Fits:** 1984–1996 XJ (with modification)
- **Gain:** Cupholders and enclosed storage — the pre-97 XJ has no cupholders from the factory
- **Fitment notes:** Can be made to fit 96 and older with minor trimming or bracket adjustment.

### XJ Overhead Console
- **Donor:** 2000–2001 XJ Cherokee (or higher-trim XJ with overhead console option)
- **Fits:** All XJ years
- **Gain:** Overhead storage and switch panel — space for aux switches, garage door openers, etc.
- **Fitment notes:** Found most commonly in 00–01 models or Limited trim. Bolt-in with existing headliner mounting points.

### Shift Console (Auto/Manual Swap)
- **Donor:** XJ Cherokee (opposite transmission type)
- **Fits:** XJ undergoing auto-to-manual or manual-to-auto conversion
- **Gain:** Correct shift console and indicator for the swapped transmission
- **Fitment notes:** Modular design — the indicator panel swaps between automatic and manual configurations.

## Floor Protection

### Police XJ Rubber Floor Liners
- **Donor:** Police-package XJ Cherokee
- **Fits:** All XJ years
- **Gain:** Rubber floor liners instead of carpet — waterproof, easy to clean
- **Fitment notes:** Direct fit. Police XJs are increasingly rare but still show up at government surplus auctions.

## Lighting

### Hella H4 Headlights
- **Donor:** Various European-market vehicles with 7" round H4 headlights
- **Fits:** All XJ years
- **Gain:** Replaceable-bulb design with better beam pattern than sealed beams
- **Fitment notes:** Any 7" round H4 housing fits the XJ headlight buckets. Hella is the most commonly recommended brand.

### GM Rearview Mirror with Map Lights
- **Donor:** Mid-1990s GM vehicles (various models)
- **Fits:** All XJ years
- **Gain:** Map lights integrated into the rearview mirror
- **Fitment notes:** Inexpensive upgrade. Requires running a power and ground wire to the mirror mount. The GM mirror base may need minor adaptation to the XJ windshield button.

### Fog Light and Switch Kit
- **Donor:** XJ Cherokee with factory fog lights
- **Fits:** XJ without factory fogs
- **Gain:** Factory fog lights with proper dash switch
- **Fitment notes:** Direct swap. Pull the lights, switch, and wiring harness as a set.

### Hatch Jamb Switch
- **Donor:** XJ Cherokee (various years)
- **Fits:** XJ with non-functioning hatch light
- **Gain:** Restores rear interior light operation when hatch opens
- **Fitment notes:** Direct replacement. Common failure point — the jamb switch corrodes and fails.

## Hatch & Cargo

### Ford Explorer Hatch Struts
- **Donor:** 1996–2000 Ford Explorer
- **Fits:** All XJ years (modification needed for 97+)
- **Gain:** Stronger hatch support struts — more durable than stock XJ units
- **Fitment notes:** Lifts the hatch about 3" lower than fully open, but the struts are much beefier and last longer. 97+ XJ may need minor bracket modification.

### OEM Swing-Out Tire Carrier
- **Donor:** 1984–1990 XJ Cherokee (with swing-out option)
- **Fits:** 1984–1990 XJ
- **Gain:** Factory rear-mounted spare tire carrier that swings out for hatch access
- **Fitment notes:** Get the carrier and the matching rear bumper as a set. Found mainly on early XJs. Not compatible with 97+ rear bumper/hatch design without significant modification.

## Trailer Wiring

### XJ Factory Trailer Wiring Harness
- **Donor:** XJ Cherokee with factory tow package
- **Fits:** All XJ years (same generation)
- **Gain:** Factory plug-in trailer wiring with proper load-sensing
- **Fitment notes:** Direct swap — plugs into the existing XJ tail-light harness. Much cleaner than aftermarket splice-in kits.

## Wheels

### 5x4.5 Bolt Pattern Wheels
- **Donor:** XJ, ZJ, TJ, YJ, Ford Explorer, Dodge Magnum Steelies, or any vehicle with 5x4.5 (5x114.3mm) bolt pattern
- **Fits:** All XJ years
- **Gain:** Wider wheel selection, different offsets, spare wheel availability
- **Fitment notes:** Any 5x4.5 wheel will bolt on. Check backspacing — XJ stock is approximately 5.5" backspacing. Going wider than stock may require trimming or fender flares.
