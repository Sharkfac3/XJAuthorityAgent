# Junkyard Upgrades — Brakes

Donor-vehicle brake components that interchange with the XJ Cherokee.
Sourced from community-compiled junkyard upgrade threads (NAXJA, JeepForum, CherokeeForam, Pirate4x4).

---

## Brake Booster & Master Cylinder

### WJ Brake Booster and Master Cylinder
- **Donor:** 1999–2004 Jeep Grand Cherokee WJ
- **Fits:** All XJ years
- **Gain:** Larger booster and master cylinder — improved pedal feel and stopping power
- **Fitment notes:** Compatible upgrade. Often paired with WJ rotors/calipers for a full front brake upgrade.

### ZJ Brake Booster and Master Cylinder
- **Donor:** 1993–1998 Jeep Grand Cherokee ZJ
- **Fits:** All XJ years
- **Gain:** Improved over early XJ (especially Renix-era) booster
- **Fitment notes:** Direct swap.

### 1995–96 XJ Power Brake Booster
- **Donor:** 1995–1996 Jeep Cherokee XJ
- **Fits:** 1987–1990 Renix-era XJ
- **Gain:** Upgraded braking over Renix-era booster
- **Fitment notes:** Direct replacement. Worthwhile upgrade for early XJs with the smaller Renix booster.

## Front Rotors & Calipers

### WJ Front Rotors and Calipers
- **Donor:** 1999–2004 Jeep Grand Cherokee WJ
- **Fits:** All XJ years
- **Gain:** Larger rotors and calipers — significantly more stopping power
- **Fitment notes:** Will fit inside 15" wheels. One of the most popular junkyard brake upgrades. Often done as a set with WJ booster/MC.

## Rear Disc Brake Conversions

### Ford Crown Victoria Rear Discs
- **Donor:** Early-to-mid 1990s Ford Crown Victoria
- **Fits:** XJ with Dana 44 or non-C-clip Dana 35
- **Gain:** Rear disc brake conversion — eliminates drums
- **Fitment notes:** Requires a spacer to adapt to XJ axle flange. Does NOT work on C-clip axles without additional modification. Best suited for D44 swaps or 8.8 builds.

### ZJ Rear Disc Brakes
- **Donor:** 1993–1998 Jeep Grand Cherokee ZJ
- **Fits:** XJ with C-clip Dana 35 or Chrysler 8.25
- **Gain:** Rear disc conversion
- **Fitment notes:** Direct conversion for C-clip D35 or 8.25 Corp axles. The go-to rear disc swap for stock-axle XJs.

### KJ Disc Brake Components
- **Donor:** 2002–2007 Jeep Liberty KJ
- **Fits:** XJ (adaptable)
- **Gain:** Rear disc conversion option
- **Fitment notes:** Adaptable setup for XJ axle integration. Less commonly documented than ZJ or Crown Vic swaps — verify fitment details before committing.
