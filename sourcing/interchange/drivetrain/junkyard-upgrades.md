# Junkyard Upgrades — Drivetrain

Donor-vehicle transmission, transfer case, and driveline components that interchange with the XJ Cherokee.
Sourced from community-compiled junkyard upgrade threads (NAXJA, JeepForum, CherokeeForam, Pirate4x4).

---

## Manual Transmission

### AX-15 from Dakota
- **Donor:** 1994+ Dodge Dakota (V6 models)
- **Fits:** XJ with manual transmission (AX-5 replacement)
- **Gain:** Stronger 5-speed — the AX-15 is a significant durability upgrade over the AX-5
- **Fitment notes:** Must use the XJ bell housing, not the Dakota's. The Dakota AX-15 internals are the same as the Jeep AX-15. Good upgrade path for 4-cylinder XJs that came with the weaker AX-5.

## Transfer Case

### NV241-OR (Rubicon Rock-Trac)
- **Donor:** 2003–2006 Jeep Wrangler TJ Rubicon only
- **Fits:** XJ (near bolt-in)
- **Gain:** 4:1 low range — dramatically better crawl ratio than the stock NP231's 2.72:1
- **Fitment notes:** Near bolt-in but requires correct input shaft and linkage adaptation. Only found in TJ Rubicons — not common at junkyards but highly sought after.

### Dodge Truck NP231 Internals
- **Donor:** Dodge truck with NP231 transfer case
- **Fits:** XJ NP231
- **Gain:** Wider chain and improved internal durability
- **Fitment notes:** Swap the internal components (chain, sprockets) from the Dodge 231 into the XJ 231 case. The Dodge version uses a wider chain. Good preventive upgrade for high-mileage or hard-use XJs.

### 5-Speed Crossmember (T-Case Drop for Autos)
- **Donor:** XJ Cherokee with manual transmission (5-speed)
- **Fits:** XJ with automatic transmission
- **Gain:** 3/4" transfer case drop with no loss in ground clearance
- **Fitment notes:** The manual-trans crossmember positions the t-case slightly differently. Using it on an auto XJ effectively lowers the t-case output angle, reducing driveshaft vibration on lifted trucks. Free if you find one at the yard.

## Driveshafts & SYE

### XJ Auto Front Driveshaft (Hack-n-Tap SYE)
- **Donor:** XJ Cherokee with automatic transmission (front driveshaft)
- **Fits:** XJ automatic (rear driveshaft replacement)
- **Gain:** Budget slip-yoke eliminator — eliminates the slip yoke at the transfer case
- **Fitment notes:** Requires a 3103-27CV yoke. The front driveshaft from an auto XJ is repurposed as the rear shaft in a hack-n-tap SYE setup. Cheap alternative to a proper SYE kit, though a real SYE is stronger.

## Transmission Cooling

### ZJ / WJ Stacked-Plate Transmission Cooler
- **Donor:** 1993–2004 Jeep Grand Cherokee ZJ or WJ
- **Fits:** All XJ years with automatic transmission
- **Gain:** Stacked-plate design — much more efficient than the stock XJ fin-style cooler
- **Fitment notes:** Comparable to a Hayden 400-series aftermarket cooler. Direct plumbing swap.

### Dodge Caravan Plate-Style Transmission Cooler
- **Donor:** Mid-1990s Dodge Caravan
- **Fits:** All XJ years with automatic transmission
- **Gain:** Plate-style cooler — significant upgrade over stock fin-style
- **Fitment notes:** Comparable to Hayden 400 series. Easy to plumb in.
