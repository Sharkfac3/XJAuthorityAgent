# Suspension & Steering Interchange Index

## Purpose
Route interchange decisions for XJ suspension, steering, and lift components.

## Scope
Donor coils, leaf springs, isolators, shackles, shocks, control arms, tierods, pitman arms, steering boxes, knuckles, and sway bars that fit the XJ from other vehicles.

## What belongs here
- donor part compatibility and fitment-decision notes
- lift-height expectations from donor springs
- modification notes for non-bolt-in parts
- links to child leaves for specific part families

## What does not belong here
- install procedures (belong under `work/upgrades/` or `work/swaps/`)
- stock suspension baselines (belong under `vehicle/systems/suspension/`)
- symptom-first troubleshooting (belongs under `diagnostics/steering-and-suspension/`)

## Child leaves
- [junkyard-upgrades.md](junkyard-upgrades.md) — community-compiled donor parts from junkyard upgrade threads
