# Junkyard Upgrades — Suspension & Steering

Donor-vehicle parts that interchange with the XJ Cherokee for suspension, steering, and lift.
Sourced from community-compiled junkyard upgrade threads (NAXJA, JeepForum, CherokeeForam, Pirate4x4).

---

## Coil Springs (Front)

### ZJ V8 Upcountry Coils
- **Donor:** 1993–1998 Jeep Grand Cherokee (V8, Upcountry/Up-Country package)
- **Fits:** All XJ years
- **Gain:** 1–1.5" lift over stock XJ coils
- **Fitment notes:** Direct swap. Used coils may yield only 0.5" due to sag. New-old-stock gives the full 1.5".

### Ford F-150 / Bronco Coils
- **Donor:** ~1979 Ford F-150 or Bronco
- **Fits:** All XJ years
- **Gain:** 4–4.5" lift
- **Fitment notes:** Very stiff ride. Must cut the coil where it bends inward. Not widely recommended since new purpose-built coils are affordable.

### Ford Thunderbird Front Coils
- **Donor:** 1983–1997 Ford Thunderbird
- **Fits:** All XJ years
- **Gain:** 3–4" lift (varies by year and trim)
- **Fitment notes:** Requires the top plate from the Thunderbird strut assembly. Lift and stiffness vary significantly across model years.

### TJ Lift Coils
- **Donor:** 1997–2006 Jeep Wrangler TJ (aftermarket lift coils)
- **Fits:** All XJ years
- **Gain:** ~1" less lift on XJ than the rated TJ lift height
- **Fitment notes:** Direct fit. A 3.5" TJ coil gives roughly 2.5" on an XJ.

## Leaf Springs (Rear)

### S10 Bastard Pack
- **Donor:** Chevrolet S10 pickup
- **Fits:** All XJ years
- **Gain:** 3–4" lift
- **Fitment notes:** Cut the eyes off the S10 main leaf and bolt the remaining pack under the XJ main leaf. Trial-and-error stacking. Cheap and common at junkyards.

### Dakota Bastard Pack
- **Donor:** Dodge Dakota
- **Fits:** All XJ years
- **Gain:** ~4" lift
- **Fitment notes:** Same fabrication method as S10 pack. Dakota leaves tend to be slightly stiffer.

### Full-Size Blazer Bastard Pack
- **Donor:** Early 1990s Chevrolet Blazer 4-door
- **Fits:** All XJ years
- **Gain:** 4–4.5" lift
- **Fitment notes:** May be stiffer than S10 leaves. Combines with stock XJ pack; can yield up to 6" total when stacked aggressively. May require shims.

## Spring Isolators

### Camaro Spring Isolators
- **Donor:** Chevrolet Camaro
- **Fits:** All XJ years (front coils)
- **Gain:** ~1.5" lift
- **Fitment notes:** Sits between coil and coil tower. Cheap spacer-style lift.

### ZJ Spring Isolators
- **Donor:** 1993–1998 Jeep Grand Cherokee ZJ
- **Fits:** All XJ years
- **Gain:** Progressive — stackable for incremental lift
- **Fitment notes:** Metal core, stronger than XJ stock rubber isolators. Can be stacked for additional height.

## Shackles

### MJ Shackles
- **Donor:** Jeep Comanche MJ
- **Fits:** All XJ years
- **Gain:** 0.5" rear lift
- **Fitment notes:** Direct replacement, bolt-in.

### GM Full-Size Pickup Shackles
- **Donor:** 1995–1999 Chevrolet/GMC C/K1500
- **Fits:** All XJ years
- **Gain:** ~1.5" rear lift
- **Fitment notes:** Also improves shackle angle at lifted ride heights.

## Shocks

### JK Rubicon Rear Shocks
- **Donor:** 2007+ Jeep Wrangler JK Rubicon
- **Fits:** XJ with 2–4" lift
- **Gain:** Longer travel shock for lifted applications
- **Fitment notes:** Must press out the JK metal sleeves and insert XJ stock bar pins. Some dispute on exact fitment — measure before committing.

### S10 Rear Shocks
- **Donor:** Chevrolet S10
- **Fits:** XJ with bastard-pack leaf lift
- **Gain:** Correct length shock for 3–4" rear lift
- **Fitment notes:** Good pairing with S10 bastard pack lift.

## Lower Control Arms (Front)

### WJ Front Lower Control Arms
- **Donor:** 1999–2004 Jeep Grand Cherokee WJ (front)
- **Fits:** All XJ years
- **Gain:** More tire clearance — boxed tubing with wider bend
- **Fitment notes:** Some grinding required for clearance. Not longer than stock — just better shaped.

### WJ Rear Lower Control Arms (as XJ Front LCAs)
- **Donor:** 1999–2004 Jeep Grand Cherokee WJ (rear)
- **Fits:** XJ with 2"+ lift
- **Gain:** 2" longer than stock XJ front LCA — corrects caster/camber on lifted XJs
- **Fitment notes:** Requires bushing modification and some grinding. Popular budget fix for lifted geometry.

### Geo Tracker / Suzuki Sidekick / Chevy Tracker LCAs
- **Donor:** 1990–1999 Geo Tracker, Suzuki Sidekick, or Chevrolet Tracker
- **Fits:** XJ with lift
- **Gain:** 2" longer than stock XJ front LCA
- **Fitment notes:** Round stock construction (not boxed). Lighter duty than WJ option.

### Dodge 2500 Upper Control Arm (as XJ Front LCA)
- **Donor:** 2004–2007 Dodge Ram 2500 (upper control arm)
- **Fits:** XJ with 3–5" lift
- **Gain:** Boxed design, correct length for medium lifts
- **Fitment notes:** Requires shim washers for bolt alignment. Good for 3–5" lift range.

### JK Rear Upper Control Arm (as XJ Front LCA)
- **Donor:** 2007+ Jeep Wrangler JK (rear upper)
- **Fits:** XJ with 1–2" lift
- **Gain:** 1–2" longer than stock XJ front LCA
- **Fitment notes:** May require bushing adaptation.

## Steering

### ZJ V8 Tierods
- **Donor:** 1993–1998 Jeep Grand Cherokee ZJ (V8 models)
- **Fits:** All XJ years
- **Gain:** Much stronger tierods — direct upgrade
- **Fitment notes:** Direct bolt-in swap. One of the most recommended junkyard upgrades.

### ZJ Pitman Arm
- **Donor:** 1993–1998 Jeep Grand Cherokee ZJ
- **Fits:** All XJ years
- **Gain:** 1" drop — helps correct steering geometry on lifted XJs
- **Fitment notes:** Direct replacement.

### Durango Steering Box
- **Donor:** Dodge Durango
- **Fits:** All XJ years
- **Gain:** Beefier steering box, better for oversized tires
- **Fitment notes:** Direct swap.

### WJ Knuckles / Tierods / Draglink (Crossover Steering)
- **Donor:** 1999–2004 Jeep Grand Cherokee WJ
- **Fits:** XJ with lift (typically 3"+)
- **Gain:** Full crossover steering conversion — much stronger than stock Y-link
- **Fitment notes:** Requires WJ knuckles, tierods, and draglink as a set. Significant upgrade but more involved than single-part swaps.

### High-Output Power Steering Pump
- **Donor:** Ford 350 or 351 engine (engine-mounted PS pump)
- **Fits:** XJ with 35"+ tires
- **Gain:** Higher-flow pump for heavy steering loads
- **Fitment notes:** Requires welding a bracket. Not bolt-in.

## Sway Bar

### ZJ V8 Front Sway Bar
- **Donor:** 1993–1998 Jeep Grand Cherokee ZJ (V8 models)
- **Fits:** All XJ years
- **Gain:** Thicker bar, improved on-road handling
- **Fitment notes:** Direct upgrade swap.

## Sway Bar End Links

### Newer XJ Sway Bar End Links
- **Donor:** Late-model XJ Cherokee
- **Fits:** Older XJ with small lift (1–1.5")
- **Gain:** 1–1.5" longer than early XJ links — clears coils on small lifts
- **Fitment notes:** Direct swap between XJ model years.

## Brake Lines (for Lifted Applications)

### YJ Brake Lines
- **Donor:** Jeep Wrangler YJ
- **Fits:** XJ with 3–4" lift
- **Gain:** 3–4" longer than stock XJ lines
- **Fitment notes:** Direct fit to XJ hard-line connections.

### Dodge Ram Van Brake Lines
- **Donor:** 1984 Dodge Ram Van
- **Fits:** XJ with 6"+ lift
- **Gain:** 10–12" longer than stock XJ lines
- **Fitment notes:** Rear lines. Essential for big lifts where stock lines are dangerously stretched.

### Dakota Rear Soft Lines
- **Donor:** 1995–2000 Dodge Dakota
- **Fits:** XJ with moderate lift
- **Gain:** ~6" longer than stock
- **Fitment notes:** Direct fit.

### GM C/K1500 Front Soft Lines
- **Donor:** 1990–1995 Chevrolet/GMC C/K1500
- **Fits:** XJ with 6"+ lift
- **Gain:** ~6" longer than stock
- **Fitment notes:** Front lines. Supports big-lift builds.

## Skid Plates

### OEM Upcountry Skid Plates
- **Donor:** XJ Cherokee with Upcountry (Up-Country) package
- **Fits:** All XJ years
- **Gain:** Factory skid plates for front, transmission, transfer case, and gas tank
- **Fitment notes:** Bolt-on. Found on higher-trim or off-road package XJs. Often cheap at junkyards (~$13 reported in CA).

### ZJ Gas Tank Skid
- **Donor:** 1993–1998 Jeep Grand Cherokee ZJ
- **Fits:** 1997+ XJ (with modification)
- **Gain:** Gas tank protection
- **Fitment notes:** Requires modification to fit 97+ XJ frame.
