# Engine Filters — Jeep Cherokee XJ

## Oil filter

The Renix and HO engine generations use different oil filter thread specs. Confirm engine year before ordering.

| Engine generation | Years | Thread |
|---|---|---|
| Renix | 1987–1990 | M20×1.5 metric |
| HO | 1991–2001 | 7/8″-11 SAE |

Fitting an HO filter onto a Renix block (or vice versa) requires a thread adapter. See [`../../../vehicle/era2/ho-engine.md`](../../../vehicle/era2/ho-engine.md) for the full filter thread context and adapter notes.

All major filter brands (Wix, Fram, Motorcraft, Purolator) offer the correct fitment by year — use the year lookup, not a universal part number.

## Air filter

Standard dry panel filter. No unusual fitment requirements across years. Replace on condition or at manufacturer's recommended interval (typically 12,000–15,000 miles in normal driving, more frequently in dusty/off-road conditions).

## Fuel filter

Inline filter mounted on the frame rail. No major year-to-year fitment differences within the XJ run. Replace on condition — a clogged fuel filter causes low fuel pressure symptoms that can be misdiagnosed as a pump failure.
