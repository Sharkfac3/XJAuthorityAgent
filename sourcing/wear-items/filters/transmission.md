# Transmission Filter — Jeep Cherokee XJ

## AW4 Automatic

The AW4 uses a drop-pan service. There is no external spin-on filter.

**Service items:**
- Filter and gasket kit (available as a combined kit from most parts suppliers)
- The pan gasket is a **flat gasket** — do not use RTV sealant
- Clean the pan of any debris before reinstalling

Order by year to confirm correct filter fitment. The AW4 was used from 1987–2001 with no major internal filter changes across that span, but confirm before ordering.

**Fluid:** See [`../fluids/transmission-fluid.md`](../fluids/transmission-fluid.md) — Dexron III/Mercon ATF.

## Manual transmissions (AX-15, AX-5, BA-10/5)

Manual transmissions do not use a serviceable filter. Service is drain-and-fill only.
