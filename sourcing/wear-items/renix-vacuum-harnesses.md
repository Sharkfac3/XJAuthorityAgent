# Renix Vacuum Harness Part Numbers

## Purpose
Sourcing reference for replacement vacuum harness assemblies on 1987–1990 Renix 4.0L XJ and MJ vehicles.

---

## Harness part numbers

| Harness | Location | NAPA part number | Dorman part number |
|---------|----------|------------------|--------------------|
| Front vacuum harness | Attaches to front of valve cover; includes grommet/fitting | BK 715-1367 | 46003 |
| Rear vacuum harness | Closest to air cleaner, EGR, etc. | BK 715-1366 | 46004 |
| Valve cover to intake manifold tube | Rear of valve cover to intake manifold; includes valve cover grommet | BK 715-1365 | 46005 |
| Throttle body to MAP hose | — | **No longer available** — see note below |

---

## Throttle body to MAP hose
This hose is no longer available as a direct replacement part. A fabricated or adapted solution is required. Consult community resources for current workarounds.
