# Coolant — Jeep Cherokee XJ

## Coolant type by year

| Year range | Type | Color |
|---|---|---|
| 1987–2000 | IAT (Inorganic Additive Technology) green antifreeze | Green |
| 2001 | HOAT (Hybrid Organic Additive Technology) | Typically orange or yellow |

**Do not use Dex-Cool.** Dex-Cool is an OAT (Organic Acid Technology) coolant. It is not compatible with IAT systems and will cause seal and gasket damage in XJs that were not designed for it.

IAT and HOAT are not compatible with each other. A complete system flush is required when switching types.

## Mix ratio

50/50 distilled water and coolant concentrate. Tap water introduces minerals that accelerate scale and corrosion — use distilled water.

## Capacity

Approximately **11–12 quarts** for the 4.0L. Exact capacity varies slightly by configuration (automatic vs. manual, heater core volume). Fill to the cold mark on the overflow bottle after bleeding the system.

## Renix era note (1987–1990)

Same coolant type applies. The thermostat housing and coolant temperature sender configuration differ from HO — see [`../../../vehicle/era1/sensors/cts.md`](../../../vehicle/era1/sensors/cts.md) before ordering sender-adjacent parts.
