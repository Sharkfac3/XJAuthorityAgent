# Transfer Case Fluid — Jeep Cherokee XJ

## NP231 (Command-Trac, 1987–2001)

**Fluid:** Dexron III / Mercon compatible ATF.

Same spec as the AW4 transmission. The NP231 uses a small internal pump to circulate fluid — gear oil is too thick for this pump, especially when cold. Do not use GL-rated gear oils.

**Capacity:** ~2.5 pints (1.2L).

---

## NP242 (Selec-Trac, 1987–2001)

**Fluid:** Dexron III / Mercon compatible ATF. The 2001 FSM updates the spec to Mopar ATF+4 (Type 9602) for that model year only.

For most XJ owners (pre-2001), Dexron III/Mercon is the factory-correct fluid. A current Dex/Merc-compatible synthetic (Mobil 1 Synthetic ATF, Valvoline MaxLife, etc.) is the community standard.

**Capacity:** ~2.85–3.0 pints (1.3–1.4L).

---

## NP207 / NP228 / NP229 (1984–1986 era)

These early transfer cases also used ATF — Dexron II was the factory spec. Use current Dexron III/Mercon-compatible ATF as the replacement.

---
