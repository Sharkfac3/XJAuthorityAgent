# Engine Oil — Jeep Cherokee XJ 4.0L

## Viscosity

| Condition | Viscosity |
|---|---|
| Standard / most climates | 10W-30 |
| Cold climates (below 0°F) | 5W-30 |
| High-mileage or worn engine | 10W-40 |

Conventional or synthetic. Synthetic is the community preference for high-mileage engines.

## Capacity

**6 quarts with filter change.**

Some filter brands seat slightly differently — fill to 5.5 quarts, run briefly, check dipstick, top to full mark.

## Change interval

| Fluid type | Interval |
|---|---|
| Conventional | 3,000–5,000 miles |
| Synthetic | 5,000–7,500 miles |

The 4.0L runs hot by design. Erring toward the shorter end of the interval is common on high-mileage engines.

## Notes

- The 2.5L four-cylinder uses the same viscosity spec and approximately the same capacity — confirm with dipstick.
- Oil filter thread differs by engine generation. Confirm before ordering — see [`../../filters/engine.md`](../../filters/engine.md).
