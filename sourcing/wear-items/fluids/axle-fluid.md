# Axle Fluid — Jeep Cherokee XJ

## Fluid spec — all stock XJ axles

**75W-90 or 80W-90 GL-5 gear oil.** These viscosity grades are equivalent in practice; either is correct. 75W-90 synthetic is a common community choice.

| Axle | Location | Capacity | Trac-Lok additive |
|---|---|---|---|
| Dana 30 | Front (4WD) | ~2.5 pints (1.2L) | Not required |
| Dana 35 | Rear | ~3.5 pints (1.7L) | 4 oz if Trac-Lok equipped |
| Chrysler 8.25 | Rear | ~4.4 pints (2.08L) | 4 oz if Trac-Lok equipped |
| Dana 44 (tow package, 1987–1989 only) | Rear | ~4.0 pints (1.9L) | 4 oz if Trac-Lok equipped |

## Trac-Lok limited slip additive

Trac-Lok is Jeep's limited-slip differential option. If the rear axle is Trac-Lok equipped, **4 oz of friction modifier / limited slip additive is required** at every fluid change. Running a Trac-Lok without the additive causes chatter and accelerates clutch wear.

Some gear oils are marketed as "limited slip compatible" or include friction modifier in the formulation. Read the label — if it does not say compatible with limited slip differentials, add the 4 oz separately.

## Tow-use note

For vehicles used to tow regularly, 75W-140 synthetic is recommended for the rear axle.
