# Cable And Ground Service Overview

## Purpose
Scope a baseline service package for battery cables, terminal ends, and major engine/chassis grounds.

## Use this package when
- battery terminals show corrosion, looseness, or heat damage
- cable insulation is stiff, swollen, oil-soaked, or green under the jacket
- the vehicle has intermittent slow-crank or low-voltage history but no confirmed failed component yet
- you are baselining an unknown-history vehicle

## Do not use this package when
- the starter does not engage and you have not yet isolated the fault
- charging voltage is low and belt, alternator, or battery condition are still unknown
- the main question is which upgrade cable kit to buy

If the symptom is active low charging voltage, start with [`diagnostics/charging-and-starting/low-charging-voltage.md`](../../../../diagnostics/charging-and-starting/low-charging-voltage.md).
