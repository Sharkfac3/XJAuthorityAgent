# Transfer Case Shifting Procedure

## Purpose
Document the correct factory procedure for shifting the NP231 transfer case on XJ and MJ vehicles between 2H, 4H, and 4L.

---

## 2H to 4H

Shift the transfer case lever from 2H to 4H while the vehicle is **moving at any legal road speed**.

Recommended technique: let off the gas, move the lever, briefly tap the gas, then let off again. This unloads the drivetrain momentarily and allows the shift to engage cleanly.

---

## 4H to 4L engagement

1. Slow the vehicle to **2–3 MPH**.
2. Shift the transmission to **Neutral**.
3. Shift the transfer case lever to the right and pull firmly rearward into **4L**.

---

## 4L disengagement

1. **Stop the vehicle completely.**
2. Shift the transmission to **Neutral**.
3. Shift the transfer case lever to **4H**.
4. Shift the transmission back to:
   - **Drive** (automatic)
   - **First gear** (manual)
5. Continue driving.

---

## Notes
- Never shift into 4L while moving — stop completely first.
- 4H can be engaged on the fly at any road speed.
- If the transfer case won't engage 4L cleanly, slight vehicle movement (rocking) while in Neutral with lever pressure applied often helps.
