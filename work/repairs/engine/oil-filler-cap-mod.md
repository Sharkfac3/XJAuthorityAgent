# Oil Filler Cap Gasket Mod

## Purpose
Stop oil weeping from the valve cover at the oil filler cap on 4.0L XJ and MJ engines by thickening the cap gasket.

## Problem
The factory oil filler cap fits loosely on the aluminum valve cover, allowing oil mist to escape and leave a film or visible leak around the cap area. The original rubber gasket alone is insufficient to seal the cap.

---

## Materials
- Small pick or screwdriver
- Bicycle inner tube (or 1/8″ Viton sheet as an alternative)
- Scissors and a knife or razor blade

---

## Procedure

1. Remove the plastic oil filler cap.
2. Use a small pick or screwdriver to wrestle the original rubber gasket out of the cap.
   - If the gasket has locking tabs that prevent removal, leave it in place and skip to step 4.
3. Trace the original gasket shape and cut a duplicate from an old bicycle inner tube using scissors and a knife or razor blade.
4. Install the duplicate gasket into the cap first, then install the original gasket on top of it (or on top of the locked-in gasket).
5. Reinstall the cap on the valve cover — it will be a tight fit the first time and may require gentle persuasion with pliers. It loosens up with use.

---

## Alternative material
1/8″ thick Viton sheet can be used in place of bicycle inner tube for a more durable custom gasket.
