# Pre-Swap Inspections Index

## Purpose
Route inspection work that decides whether a stock or unknown-condition Jeep is healthy enough before a major swap.

## Scope
Use this branch for pre-swap condition checks, baseline documentation, and go/no-go inspection workflows before control-system or drivetrain conversion work begins.

## What belongs here
- pre-swap health checks
- baseline measurements to record before teardown or conversion
- inspection checklists that support swap planning
- links to deeper diagnostics when the vehicle fails inspection

## What does not belong here
- full swap procedures
- symptom-first fault trees as the primary content
- stock reference with no inspection action
- donor-part or kit shopping guidance

## Child branches
- `era3-health-check.md` — 1996–2001 stock-control-system baseline checks before ECU swap work

## Transition note
This branch exists to receive inspection workflow material split out of legacy mixed-purpose files such as `vehicle/era3/diagnostics.md`.
