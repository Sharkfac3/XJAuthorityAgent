# EV6 / USCAR: Injector Application

## Pinout
| Pin | Circuit |
|---|---|
| 1 | Injector power (12V from ASD relay) |
| 2 | Injector ground (ECU low-side control) |

## Compatibility with aftermarket injectors
EV6/USCAR is the current industry standard. The widest selection of modern performance injectors uses EV6. If upgrading injectors on a 1999–2001 XJ, EV6 aftermarket options are abundant.

## Adapter to EV1 harness
If retrofitting EV6 injectors onto an EV1 harness (1988–1998 vehicles):
- EV1-to-EV6 adapter pigtails available from multiple suppliers
- Wiring is pin-for-pin identical — power to power, ground to ground
- No electrical modification required — adapter is purely mechanical
- Six adapters needed for a full set

## Release procedure
1. Disconnect battery negative
2. Squeeze side-release clip while pulling connector straight off
3. No special tool required — clip is accessible with fingers on most installations
