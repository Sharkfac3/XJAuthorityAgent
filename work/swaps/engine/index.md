# Engine Swap Index

## Purpose
Route engine-family swap projects for XJ and MJ vehicles.

## Scope
Full engine replacement with a different family, variant, or generation of engine — including block swaps within the 4.0L family across Renix and HO eras.

## What belongs here
- engine-family swap procedure leaves
- compatibility notes that only apply in the context of this swap class
- required-parts and validation stubs specific to engine swaps

## What does not belong here
- ECU or engine-management architecture changes (→ `work/swaps/ecu/`)
- transmission or drivetrain swaps without an accompanying engine change
- stock baseline engine facts (→ `vehicle/`)
- symptom-first diagnostics
