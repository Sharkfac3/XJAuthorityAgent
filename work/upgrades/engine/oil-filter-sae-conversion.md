# Oil Filter SAE Thread Conversion

## Purpose
Replace the proprietary 20mm-threaded oil filter nipple on Renix-era (1987–1990) XJ and MJ engines with a standard 3/4″ SAE-threaded nipple, enabling the use of widely available oil filters.

---

## Part

| Item | Part number | Source |
|------|-------------|--------|
| Oil filter nipple assembly | **53007563AB** | Dealer (under $10) or 1991+ XJ salvage |

Only the nipple is replaced — the adapter housing remains on the engine block.

---

## Tools

| Model year | Wrench size for removal |
|------------|------------------------|
| 1987–1990 (Renix) | 7/8″ |
| 1991+ | 15/16″ |

**Note:** Space constraints may require a deep-well socket or a 1″ regular socket instead of the specified wrench size.

Apply penetrating oil before removal if the nipple is seized.
