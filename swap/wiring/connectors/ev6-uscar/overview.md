# EV6 / USCAR: Overview

## Identity
- Family: USCAR EV6 (Bosch EV6 / Delphi Multec 2)
- Pin count: 2 (signal + ground)
- Body: Narrow rectangular, ~12mm wide — noticeably slimmer than EV1
- Locking tab: Side-mounted clip
- Gender: Female on harness side

## Years used on XJ
- Era 3 (1999–2001): All injectors
- Not used on Era 1, Era 2, or Era 3 pre-1999

## Why it changed
The EV6/USCAR connector became the industry standard in the late 1990s. Chrysler adopted it for the 1999 model year refresh.

## Terminal
- Type: USCAR female terminal
- Wire range: 18–20 AWG

## Release tool
Side-release clip requires a small flat pick. Less brittle than EV1 — ages better. Still inspect condition before reuse.

## Sourcing
- New pigtails: Widely available — search "EV6 injector connector pigtail" or "USCAR injector pigtail"
- EV1-to-EV6 adapter: Available if retrofitting newer EV6 injectors onto an older EV1 harness
