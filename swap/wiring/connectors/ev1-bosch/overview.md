# EV1 / Bosch Jetronic: Overview

## Identity
- Family: Bosch Jetronic EV1
- Pin count: 2 (signal + ground)
- Body: Wide rectangular, ~18mm wide
- Locking tab: Top-mounted, single tab
- Seal: Individual wire seals per cavity
- Gender: Female on harness side

## Years used on XJ
- Era 1 (1988–1990): All injectors
- Era 2 (1991–1995): All injectors
- Era 3 (1996–1998): All injectors
- Era 3 (1999–2001): Replaced by EV6 — see `ev6-uscar/`

## Age and condition
EV1 connectors on XJs are 25–35 years old. The connector body becomes brittle with heat cycling. The locking tab is the first thing to fail. Inspect all 6 before reuse. If any tab is cracked or missing, replace the connector pigtail — a loose injector connector causes intermittent misfires that are difficult to diagnose.

## Terminal
- Type: Bosch Jetronic female terminal
- Material: Brass, tin-plated
- Wire range: 18–20 AWG

## Release tool
A dedicated EV1 removal pick is required to depress the locking tab without breaking the connector body. Do not use a screwdriver — it will crack the housing. Removal pick available from most automotive tool suppliers.

## Sourcing
- New connector pigtails: Widely available. Search "EV1 injector connector pigtail"
- Pre-made repair harness (all 6): Available from XJ specialty suppliers
- EV1-to-EV6 adapter: Available if upgrading to EV6 injectors
